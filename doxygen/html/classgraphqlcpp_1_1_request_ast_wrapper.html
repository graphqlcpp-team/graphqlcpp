◆ extractResolver()

ResolverInfo graphqlcpp::RequestAstWrapper::extractResolver ( )

This function extracts the resolverInfo. The resolve Info is consisting of the resolver name and arguments. The resolver name is the name of the selectionSet of the rootNodeQuery. The resolver arguments are ResolverArguments. These are consisting of the name and the value. These are extracted out of the arguments of the selectionSet of the rootNodeQuery.
